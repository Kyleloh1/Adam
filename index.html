Adam Dynamics Visualizer

Side-by-side 3-D phase portraits of position \(x'\), first moment \(m'\), and second moment \(v'\), in rescaled coordinates.

Loss. Adam is run on the quadratic loss \[ f(x)=\tfrac12\,x^\top A x,\qquad \nabla f(x)=A x, \] where the loss matrix is a diagonal spectrum rotated by three Euler angles, \[ A=Q\,\Lambda\,Q^\top,\quad \Lambda=\operatorname{diag}(\lambda_1,\lambda_2,\lambda_3),\quad Q=R_z(\theta_z)R_y(\theta_y)R_x(\theta_x). \]

Map (unscaled coordinates). With learning rate \(h\), decays \(\beta_1,\beta_2\), stabilizer \(\varepsilon\) (all component-wise): \[ m_{n+1}=\beta_1 m_n+(1-\beta_1)\nabla f(x_n),\quad v_{n+1}=\beta_2 v_n+(1-\beta_2)\big(\nabla f(x_n)\big)^2, \] \[ x_{n+1}=x_n-\dfrac{h\,m_{n+1}}{\sqrt{v_{n+1}}+\varepsilon}. \]
Map (rescaled coordinates). Writing \(x'=x/h\), \(m'=m/h\), \(v'=\sqrt{v}/h\), the same dynamics become \[ m'_{n+1}=\beta_1 m'_n+(1-\beta_1)\nabla f(x'_n),\qquad v'_{n+1}=\sqrt{\,\beta_2\,v_n'^{2}+(1-\beta_2)\big(\nabla f(x'_n)\big)^2\,}, \] \[ x'_{n+1}=x'_n-\dfrac{m'_{n+1}}{v'_{n+1}+\varepsilon/h}. \] The plots and initial-condition inputs use these rescaled variables \((x',m',v')=(x/h,\ m/h,\ \sqrt{V}/h)\). The learning-rate field is in units of \(10^{-6}\).
